About Us

Who We Are

Based in Wen'an Town, Zhijiang City, we are a trading company dedicated to bridging local producers with broader markets. Our name reflects a commitment to steady growth — like roots reaching deep into fertile soil.

From our office on Jinquan Street, we coordinate sourcing, distribution, and partnership development with a focus on transparency and long-term value.
